SECTION A - COLLISION COVERAGE
Clause ID: COL-101
=========================================================

We pay for direct and accidental loss to your covered vehicle caused by
collision with another vehicle or object.

Deductible:
$500

=========================================================
SECTION B - COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
Clause ID: COMP-201
=========================================================

We pay for direct physical loss caused by:

- Fire
- Theft
- Hail
- Flood
- Vandalism
- Falling objects
- Animal collision
- Windstorm
- Glass damage

Deductible:
$250

=========================================================
SECTION C - WINDSHIELD COVERAGE
Clause ID: GLASS-301
=========================================================

Windshield repair:
$0 deductible

Windshield replacement:
$100 deductible

=========================================================
SECTION D - RENTAL REIMBURSEMENT
Clause ID: RENT-401
=========================================================

Rental reimbursement is available after a covered loss.

Maximum:
$50 per day

Maximum duration:
30 days

=========================================================
SECTION E - TOWING
Clause ID: TOW-501
=========================================================

Emergency roadside towing is covered up to $200.

=========================================================
SECTION F - MEDICAL PAYMENTS
Clause ID: MED-601
=========================================================

Medical payments coverage:

Maximum:
$10,000 per injured insured occupant.

=========================================================
SECTION G - OEM PARTS
Clause ID: OEM-701
=========================================================

Repairs during the first 3 years must use OEM parts.

=========================================================
SECTION H - EXCLUDED DRIVER
Clause ID: EXCL-801
=========================================================

Michael Smith is listed as an excluded driver.

No coverage applies while the excluded driver operates
the insured vehicle.

=========================================================
SECTION I - RACING EXCLUSION
Clause ID: EXCL-802
=========================================================

No coverage applies while participating in racing,
timed competition,
or speed contests.

=========================================================
SECTION J - MECHANICAL BREAKDOWN
Clause ID: EXCL-803
=========================================================

Mechanical failure,
electrical failure,
engine failure,
wear and tear,
and deterioration are NOT covered unless directly caused
by a covered collision or comprehensive loss.

=========================================================
SECTION K - FRAUD
Clause ID: EXCL-804
=========================================================

Coverage is void if the insured intentionally conceals
or misrepresents material facts.

=========================================================
SECTION L - DUTIES AFTER LOSS
Clause ID: COND-901
=========================================================

The insured must:

• Report the loss promptly.
• Protect the vehicle from further damage.
• Provide photographs when available.
• Cooperate with the investigation.
• Provide repair estimates upon request.
